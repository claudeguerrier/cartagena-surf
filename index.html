About Cartagena Surf Experience

Founded by Jhovanny Cotta Correa, Cartagena Surf Experience was born from a passion for surfing and a deep love for the beautiful coastline of Colombia. What started as a small rental shop has grown into Cartagena's premier surf school.

Our mission is to share our love of surfing with both locals and visitors, providing safe, fun, and professional surf instruction while promoting respect for the ocean and the local environment.

Jhovanny Cotta Correa

Head Instructor & Founder
